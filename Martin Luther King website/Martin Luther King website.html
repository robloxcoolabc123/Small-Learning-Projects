Martin Luther King Timeline

1929

Martin Luther King was born in Atlanta, Georgia, USA.

1935

When Martin was 6 years old he was told his white friend couldn't play with him anymore.

1945

MLK was very smart so started college a year earlier than most children his age.

1954

MLK became a minister.

1955

MLK earned his degree and became Doctor Martin Luther King.

1956

MLK organised peaceful bus boycott after Rosa Parks refused to give up her seat.

1956

MLK house was bombed.

28th August 1963

MLK gave his famous "I have a dream speech" in Washington, USA.

1964

MLK was the youngest man to win the Nobel Prize.

1966

MLK married Coretta Scott.

1968

Martin Luther King was assassinated in Memphis Tennesse, outside his motel room.

1986

MLK day declared a national holiday.

Martin Luther King Facts

  1. Martin Luther King Jr is remembered for his tireless work during the
    Civil Rights Movement and his dream that one day everyone would be treated as equals.
    A statue built in his memory now stands in Washington D.C. and each year,
    he third Monday in January is celebrated as Martin Luther King Jr. Day, an American federal holiday.

  2. King was arrested in 1963 for protesting the treatment of blacks in Birmingham.

  3. The civil rights march from Selma to Montgomery, Alabama, in 1965.
